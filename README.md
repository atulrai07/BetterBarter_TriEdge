## 🚀 Overview

BetterBarter is a **trust-based marketplace** that enables people to exchange **skills and surplus goods** using a **credit system instead of money**.

It empowers local communities to **collaborate, share, and grow together**.

---

## ⚠️ Problem

> People in local neighborhoods have valuable skills and resources, but lack a trusted system to safely exchange them within their community.

---

## 💡 Solution

BetterBarter introduces a **Social Capital Economy** where:

- 🔁 Skills and goods are exchanged directly  
- 💳 Credits replace money  
- ⭐ Trust score builds credibility  
- 📍 Local discovery ensures safety  

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔧 Skill & Goods Exchange
- Post requests (repair, tutoring, etc.)
- Offer skills or surplus goods

### 💳 Credit-Based Economy
- Earn credits by helping others  
- Spend credits to get services  

### ⭐ Trust Score System
- Ratings after each trade  
- Builds user credibility  

### 📍 Location-Based Discovery
- Find nearby requests and offers  

### 💬 Messaging System
- Communicate before & during trades  

### 🔒 Safe Interaction
- Meeting preferences (Safe spot / Home visit)  
- Transparent trade history
